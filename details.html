Massive open online courses (MOOCs)

Technology has revolutionized learning. The rapid growth of economical technology brings to the world a massive opportunity for learning. Online learning is an effective solution to many- those who are unable to pursue their academic degree due to work, those whose education is hampered due to limited seat constraints in a traditional education system, and to everyone who are hungry for learning.

INTERNSHIPS

Students today are learning how to be smart workers, balancing their academics with improving newer skills. And internships are an amazing way to do so. Internships incredibly help a student improve. You can learn new skills in your internship, you can explore newer work environments, and internships also give you a chance to try and figure out what you like doing and what you don’t. Students gain experience, and internships also look very good on a resume to prospective employers.
